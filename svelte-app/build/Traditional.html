Traditional

I have been doing traditional artwork for most of my life since i was very young. I have
always had a big passion for drawing and over the years have started experimenting with
different styles and materials. My favourite type of drawings to do is cartoon style using a lot
of colour with markers and pencils. Here i will show some of my traditional drawings!
My art style has always been very weird and unusual drawings, very trippy stuff with a lot of
bright colours. I have done some realistic drawings as you can see but my main style is the
weird and colourful ones.
